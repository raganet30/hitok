Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I create a HiTok account?

Creating a HiTok account is simple:

  1. Download the HiTok app from App Store or Google Play
  2. Open the app and tap "Register"
  3. Choose a nickname and enter your details
  4. Verify your phone number or email
  5. Set up your password
How do I add money to my HTC Wallet?

You can top up your HTC Wallet through:

  • GCash: Scan the QR code in the app
  • Payment Centers: Visit authorized payment centers
  • Bank Transfer: Send to our designated bank accounts

Minimum top-up amount is ₱50.
