Where can I download HiTok?

You can download the HiTok app from:

Playstore (For Android Users)

  1. Open your Google Playstore
  2. Search for HiTok
  3. Click The HiTok Logo
  4. Click Install

Appstore (For iOS or Apple User)

Directlink (For devices connected to HiWiFi Networks)

How can I create a new Account?
  1. Open your HiTok application
  2. Click the "Go in" button
  3. Enter your desired nickname
  4. (Optional) Enter your recommendation code
  5. Bind your Phone number or email address
How can I Log-in on HiTok?
  1. Open your HiTok application
  2. Click the - button
  3. Select to log in via Phone Number or Email Address
  4. Enter your Phone Number or Email Address
  5. Enter your Password
How can I bind my phone number or email address?
  1. Open and Log-in your HiTok Application
  2. Click the Profile Icon at the bottom corner
  3. Click on Settings
  4. Click Account Security
  5. Click either Phone Number or Email Address
  6. Enter your details
  7. Click Send Verification Code
  8. Once Code is received, typed it in the verification bar
  9. Type in your Log-in Password
  10. Click Confirm
How can I change my password?
  1. Open and Log-in your HiTok Application
  2. Click the Profile Icon at the bottom corner
  3. Click on Settings
  4. Click Account Security
  5. Click Change Log-in Password
  6. Type in Original Password
  7. Type in new password and re-enter it
  8. Click Confirm
How can I edit my profile?
  1. Open and Log-in your HiTok Application
  2. Click the Profile button at the bottom corner
  3. Click your Name
  4. Click Dating Profile
  5. Click Edit button at the top corner
  6. Click the information you want to edit
How can I change my nickname?
  1. Open and Log-in your HiTok Application
  2. Click the Profile button at the bottom corner
  3. Click Settings
  4. Click Account Security
  5. Type in new nickname
  6. Click Save at the top corner
How can I change my profile picture?
  1. Open and Log-in your HiTok Application
  2. Click the Profile button at the bottom corner
  3. Click Settings
  4. Click Account Security
  5. Click the three dots at the top corner
  6. Click Album and select desired photo
  7. Click Done
What is HTC?

HTC or HiTok coin is the HiTok Application currency you can use in order to:

  1. Transfer Money
  2. Select a Traffic Data or Internet Package
  3. Purchasing and Transaction
How can I top up in HiTok?
  1. Open and Log-in your HiTok Application
  2. Click the Profile button at the bottom corner
  3. Click Wallet
  4. Click Top up
  5. Type in or select the amount you want to top up
  6. Click Confirm
  7. Save the QR and pay at your G-cash
  8. Open your G-cash
  9. Click the QR at the bottom center
  10. Upload your HiTok recharge QR code
  11. Successful payment once receipt is confirmed!
How can I Transfer HTC?

Option 1

  1. Open and Log-in your HiTok Application
  2. Click the Profile button at the bottom corner
  3. Click Wallet
  4. Click Transfer
  5. Type your friend account number or bind mobile number
  6. Type in the amount you want to transfer
  7. Click Confirm

Option 2

  1. Open and Log-in your HiTok Application
  2. Click the Profile button at the bottom corner
  3. Click the QR Icon at the top corner
  4. Click Scan
  5. Scan the QR Code of your friend
  6. Click Transfer
  7. Type the amount of HTC you want to transfer
  8. Click Confirm
What E-Wallet partner can we use to top-up?
  • Gcash Topup
  • Fast Topup
How can I check my points from referral bonus?
  1. Refer a friend using your Referral Code or Referral Link
  2. Make sure Referred friend bind a Phone number or Email Address
  3. Open and Log-in your HiTok Application
  4. Click the Profile Icon at the bottom corner
  5. Click Points to see successful referral
How can I convert my points from referral bonus?
  1. Open and Log-in your HiTok Application
  2. Click the Profile Icon at the bottom corner
  3. Click Points
  4. Click Point Conversion
  5. Enter the amount of Point you want to convert
  6. Click Confirm
How can I get my free 45 minute access?

Option 1

  1. Connect your device to the HiiWiFi Network
  2. Open and Log-in your HiTok Application
  3. Click the Popped up 45 minutes at the bottom center
  4. Click 45 Minutes
  5. Click Confirm
  6. Enjoy your free 45 minutes free access

Option 2

  1. Connect your device to the HiiWiFi Network
  2. Open and Log-in your HiTok Application
  3. Click the Traffic Data icon at the bottom center
  4. Click Voucher Order
  5. Click 45 Minutes
  6. Click Confirm
  7. Enjoy your free 45 minutes free access

Option 2
